~Mystery Data~

How well do you know your data? Test yourself, your friends or your colleagues. Upload a csv to start! Ideally choose one with more than a couple of fields and roll the dice to see if you can define what the field means.

Try five mysteries! If at least 3 have you stumped, consider creating documentation or a data dictionary on where the data comes from and what it represents! Don't let data be misunderstood.

Some notes about the data mystery game above.
  1. This game was inspired by this tweet by @thejunglejane and was remixed from Glitch's d3 CSV parse challenge app.
  2. Any CSV you upload is processed on the client side only (aka is not saved). This means that you can't share a link with a friend _after_ you've uploaded, but wouldn't it be more fun to play together anyway? But for real, this is mostly so I didn't need to worry about any file security issuess.
  3. After about ~8 years of working in data companies or on data-related teams, I both 1) distrust all data and 2) believe in documentation, even if it's just for you.
  4. Made by @eveahe, all suggestions for improvement welcome! You can remix 4 your own data game.
