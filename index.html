~Mystery Data~

How well do you know your data? Test yourself, your friends or your colleagues. Upload a csv to start! Ideally choose one with more than a couple of fields and roll the dice to see if you can define what the field means.

See if you can solve five mysteries and if at least 3 have you stumped, consider creating documentation or a data dictionary on where the data comes from and what it represents! Don't let data be misunderstood.
